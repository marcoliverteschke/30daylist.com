30daylist

The last impulse buy you’ll ever make.*

Capture

You’re at the store and you see something you like. But instead of reaching for your wallet, you grab your iPhone, launch 30daylist, and quickly enter the basic information of the thing you saw.
30daylist even captures your current location, so you can find your way back to the dusty little bookstore in that hard-to-find alley.

Forget

Once it’s in the appâ€¦ just forget about it. In a month 30daylist will remind you with a notification and a badge on the icon, that finally the time has come to make your decision.

Review

30 days are up? Now it’s time to decide.
You've had time to make up your mind. Do you really still want that thing? Can you afford it? Heck, did you save-up for it? 30daylist can open a store-URL for you of show you on a map, where exactly you saw that thing.
Or maybe you decided that you don’t actually need it. No problem, just swipe to delete the entry. Done.
